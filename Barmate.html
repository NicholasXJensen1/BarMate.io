About Us

BarMate is a smart phone app that utilizes location based services to provide social opportunities for individuals to interact at physical locations.

BarMate is a company started by six (6) young adults. The original idea for BarMate was much like the idea for the telephone where two people had almost the exact same idea at the same time. Two of our co-founders, had this idea that people at bars spend much time thinking about interacting with people and too little time doing so. Two friends in Tallahassee, Florida contacted a long time friend in Akron, Ohio, Rodney Morgan. Rodney assembled the rest of the founding team and when it all became official, five of the six original members were able to move forward with product creation. We now have a sound team around a remarkable product, "The Guide to Going Out!"

BarMate for Business

When it comes to running a business, you can never have enough data on your market. BarMate for Business is the perfect data analytics tool for bar owners!

×

With BarMate for Business, bar owners will be able to update and market specials, events, and unique bar attributes, but they will get much more than that! Bar owners will have the ability to see metrics on their specials and special events. BarMate will track the influx of users during special events and specials to truly show how successful each event is. Bar owners can see this data and better understand their audience. With this tool bars can have much more successful promotions.
